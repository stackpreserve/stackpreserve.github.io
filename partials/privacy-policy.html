Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

StackPreserve ("we", "our", or "us") operates a session replay analytics platform designed for indie developers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (email address, login details)
  • Usage data (pages visited, clicks, interactions)
  • Device and browser information
  • Technical logs and performance data

2. Session Replay Data

Our service records user interactions on websites that integrate our tracking script. This may include:

  • Mouse movements and clicks
  • Page navigation
  • Scroll behavior

We do not intentionally collect sensitive data such as passwords, payment information, or personal identifiers. Developers are responsible for masking sensitive fields.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Analyze user behavior
  • Debug and optimize websites
  • Ensure platform security

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only:

  • With service providers (hosting, infrastructure)
  • If required by law

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect data, but no system is completely secure.

6. Your Responsibilities

  • Informing your users about tracking
  • Obtaining consent where required
  • Avoiding collection of sensitive data

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use means acceptance of changes.
